# Onbillo — Next-Gen Billing & POS for Indian Retail

Onbillo is a multi-platform billing & POS system for Indian retail shops (kirana stores, restaurants, wholesale dealers, supermarkets, and boutiques). It combines barcode scanning, thermal printing, GST compliance, role-based access, cloud sync with offline-first resilience, and a community-powered global product database.

---

## Core Capabilities

### 1. Barcode Scanner
Scan any barcode using your phone camera or a hardware scanner. Product details are filled automatically, enabling you to create bills in seconds and minimizing manual errors.
- **Benefits:** Faster checkout, fewer mistakes, and a better customer experience.

### 2. Reports
View daily, weekly, and monthly sales reports. Track revenue, top-selling products, and cashier performance from a single dashboard.
- **Benefits:** Real-time sales tracking, profit analysis, and data-backed business decisions.

### 3. GST Compliance
Generate professional GST-compliant invoices with automated tax calculations, reducing billing mistakes and simplifying GST filing.
- **Benefits:** Professional invoices, automatic tax calculations, and easier accounting.

### 4. Analytics
Discover top-selling items, busiest hours, and overall business trends using visual charts and clean statistics.
- **Benefits:** Track business growth, identify popular stock, and improve purchasing decisions.

### 5. Printer Support
Connect thermal printers and print professional receipts instantly. Supports most Bluetooth and USB POS printers.
- **Benefits:** Fast receipt printing, universal printer compatibility, and better customer service.

### 6. Offline Support
Continue billing and managing your store even during internet outages. All offline data automatically syncs to the cloud once the connection is restored.
- **Benefits:** Zero business downtime, reliable offline checkout, and automatic cloud synchronization.

---

## Global Barcode Database
Access a community-powered global product database. When you scan a new item, Onbillo automatically pulls its name, image, and details, removing the need to manually register every SKU.

---

## Simple Setup in Four Steps
1. **Create Your Shop:** Register your business, add shop details, and customize your billing preferences.
2. **Add Products:** Import your product list or scan barcodes. Existing database items are auto-populated.
3. **Start Billing:** Scan items, generate invoices, print receipts, and track stock in real time.
4. **Grow Your Business:** Use automated reports and sales analytics to scale your operations.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

- **Can this make billing faster?**
  Yes. Simply scan a product barcode, adjust the quantity, and generate the bill in seconds.
- **Will I lose my existing data?**
  No. Your data is stored securely and backed up regularly.
- **Is it easy to learn?**
  Yes. Onbillo is designed for ease of use by owners and staff with minimal training.
- **Does it work offline?**
  Yes. You can continue billing offline, and it will sync once you are back online.
- **Can my workers use it?**
  Yes. Create accounts with custom roles (Owner, Manager, Cashier) and permissions.
- **Is it GST compliant?**
  Yes. Invoices are GST-ready with automated tax calculations.
